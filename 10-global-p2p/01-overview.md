# Global P2P

Send and receive low-cost real-time payments to bank accounts and UMA addresses worldwide through a single, simple API.

Grid automatically routes each payment across its network of Grid switches, handling FX, blockchain settlement, and instant banking off-ramps for you.

## Features

### Single API, Global Reach
Grid interacts with the Money Grid to route your payments globally.

### No Crypto Handling
Leverages local instant banking rails and global low latency crypto rails to settle payments in real-time.

### Instant Delivery
Leverages local instant banking rails and global low latency crypto rails to settle payments in real-time.

## Payment Flow

1. **Fund Internal Account** or prepare for Just-in-Time Funding
2. **Create Quote** - Lock exchange rate to the receiving foreign account
3. **Execute Quote** - Initiate the transfer

## UMA (Universal Money Address)

For background on the UMA protocol itself, see the UMA Standard documentation: https://docs.uma.me/uma-standard/introduction

UMA enables instant, low-cost global payments using email-like addresses: `$user@domain.com`

## Use Cases

- **Cross-Border Remittance** - Send money person-to-person across borders
- **UMA Payments** - Send money using Universal Money Addresses
- **Wallet-to-Wallet** - Transfer between crypto wallets globally
- **Freelancer Payments** - Pay freelancers internationally
- **Family Support** - Send money to family abroad

## Key Differences from Payouts

Global P2P focuses on:
- UMA address payments
- Individual-to-individual transfers
- Lightning-fast crypto settlement
- Lower fees for small amounts
